Overview

Sometimes, songs are too high or too low. If that is the case, you should change the key of the song. The key of a song decides what pitches to sing. A high key will have higher pitches than a lower key. If you want to sing the key at a lower key, take all the chords in the song and change them into a lower chord, as dictated by the scale below. If you want to sing a higher key, take the chords and change them into a higher chord.

Scale

Click on a chord to view the other chords relative to that chord. To lower a chord by 2 steps, for example, your new chord will be under "-2" after clicking on the original chord. To lower the key of a song, do that process for every chord in the song, or use the Mass Transpose app below.

This scale should extend from -6 to +6. You may have to scroll if your screen is too small.
